Welcome To Athena Academy Boarding School

Located in Johannesburg, Athena Academy Boarding School is a hub that welcomes girls from across Africa. We value empowerment, excellence, and innovation, fostering a culture where young women are encouraged to lead with confidence and integrity. With a focus on law, STEAM, and the arts, we promote academic rigor, creative exploration, and personal growth, ensuring our students reach their full potential. Our diverse, inclusive community thrives on sisterhood, collaboration, and respect, preparing students to become future leaders. Through holistic development, leadership opportunities, and community engagement, Athena Academy nurtures well-rounded individuals who are ready to make a meaningful impact on the world.

About Us

Founded in 2020 by billionaire philanthropist Hazel Sibhuku, Athena Academy Boarding School is a scholarship-based institution committed to providing a world-class education for young African women. With a unique pay-what-you-can model, the school is maintained entirely through donations, ensuring that every contribution directly supports staff salaries and the continuous improvement of the school. Open to all African nationals, Athena Academy is proud to offer an inclusive and diverse learning environment with no religious affiliation. We believe in fostering a community where students thrive academically, creatively, and personally.

Our curriculum stands apart by shining a spotlight on women’s contributions throughout history, science, and the arts. Subjects like history, painting, music, writing, biology, law, physics, calculus, and coding are taught by highly educated female instructors who are experts in their fields. We encourage self-sufficiency by engaging students in practical life skills such as assisting female chefs, tending to the school’s garden, and helping with livestock for milk and eggs. Chores are shared and rotated among students, cultivating a sense of responsibility and community.

At Athena Academy, we foster leadership, creativity, and personal growth. Students participate in outdoor learning experiences, from science experiments to physical education and artistic activities. The school uniform is designed for comfort and freedom of movement, encouraging an active lifestyle. Clubs are student-led, empowering girls to explore their interests. Our mentorship program pairs each student with a younger peer, fostering a supportive sisterhood that builds confidence and camaraderie across grade levels.

Our Values

  • Innovation: The academy fosters a culture of curiosity and innovation. We encourage our students to think critically, solve problems creatively, and embrace cutting-edge technology as they prepare for the ever-changing global landscape.

  • Holistic Growth: Athena Academy is dedicated to the personal development of every student. We balance intellectual rigor with emotional, social, and physical well-being, ensuring that our students grow into well-rounded individuals.

Our Values are clearly shown on our uniform. The school uniform is designed for mobility, featuring earth-toned loose pants and shirts that encourage freedom of movement and eliminate the worry of getting dirty during hands-on activities.

Events Page

A Visit From Beyonce!

Athena Academy is excited to welcome Beyoncé to campus for a special talk! As a global icon in music, fashion, and activism, Beyoncé will share her journey, touching on leadership, resilience, and the power of creativity. She'll speak directly to our students, offering advice on how young women can pursue their dreams and make an impact. Following her talk, there will be a brief Q&A session where students can engage with her and ask questions. This is a unique opportunity for our students to connect with a leader who embodies the values we teach at Athena Academy.

Girls Sewing Day!

Athena Academy is hosting a Stitching Skills Workshop where experienced students will teach beginners basic sewing skills. Through hands-on demonstrations, older students will guide younger ones in tasks like threading needles and creating simple patterns. This workshop fosters mentorship, teamwork, and practical skill-building in a fun, supportive environment.

A Visit From Zahra Hassan!

Athena Academy is excited to host Zahra Hassan, also known as MissWondrousoul on Instagram, for an engaging and insightful event. A primary school teacher and digital creator, Zahra is known for her unorthodox teaching methods and strong advocacy for teacher mental health. She will share her experiences in balancing creativity in the classroom with self-care, emphasizing the importance of mental well-being for both educators and students. Students and staff will have the chance to learn from Zahra’s innovative approaches to education and her inspiring journey in creating supportive learning environments. This event is a unique opportunity to explore fresh perspectives on teaching and well-being.
